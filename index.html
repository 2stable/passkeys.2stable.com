What Are Passkeys

Passkeys are a next-generation authentication technology designed to replace passwords. Supported by the FIDO Alliance, a coalition of over 400 companies including Google, Microsoft, and Apple, passkeys offer a more secure and convenient way to protect your accounts.

Why Passkeys Are Important

Traditional password-based authentication is vulnerable to various attacks, including phishing, brute force attempts, and password reuse. These weaknesses can make it easier for hackers to access your accounts without permission.

Passkeys improve security by using public-key cryptography to create a unique key pair for each user. This makes it much harder for attackers to breach the authentication process, providing a more secure way to protect your accounts.
