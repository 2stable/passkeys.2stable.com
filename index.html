What are Passkeys#

Passkeys are a next-generation authentication technology that replaces passwords with public-key cryptography. Supported by the FIDO Alliance, a group of over 400 companies including Apple, Google, and Microsoft, passkeys offer a safer and more convenient way to protect your accounts.

Why Passkeys are important#

Traditional password-based authentication is vulnerable to various attacks, including phishing, brute force.
Passkeys improve security by using public-key cryptography to create a unique key pair for each user. This makes it much harder for attackers to breach the authentication process, providing a more secure way to protect your accounts.
