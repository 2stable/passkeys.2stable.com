How do I set up the Passkeys App by 2Stable as the main passkeys app on my device?

Starting with iOS 17, Apple allows you to choose the main passkeys app from your device settings. Follow these simple steps to set it up.

By setting the Passkeys App by 2Stable as your main, your device can automatically suggest our app when you’re setting up passkeys for your accounts. This streamlines the process and keeps your logins secure and organized.

Choose the guide below based on your device:

  • Set as Default on iPhone or iPad

  • Set as Default on Mac

ℹ️ Note: Once set, your device may open the Passkeys App automatically during passkeys setup, helping you skip extra steps.
